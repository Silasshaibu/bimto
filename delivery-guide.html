Delivery times

UK delivery of standard products

Most standard orders to the UK are delivered for £3.75 or less, many are free. The delivery cost is based on the product’s size and weight.

If a product is delivered free of charge this is clearly displayed on the product page.

UK delivery of non-standard products

A non-standard product is something that’s considered to be fragile, perishable, high value, large, heavy, or something that has exceptional delivery requirements. For these products, the above costs won’t apply – you’ll find the delivery cost on the product page.

UK tracked express delivery

If you need something quickly, many maker/sellers offer Tracked Express Delivery to UK destinations. The product will be delivered the next working day after it has been dispatched.

International delivery

International delivery is available on many products and is displayed on the product page. The cost for delivery is calculated based on the destination, size and the weight of the product.

Import Duties and Customs:

When ordering to a country outside of the UK, your items may be subject to import duties and taxes which are levied when the delivery reaches the specified destination. You will be responsible for payment of any such import duties and taxes. Please note that we have no control over these charges and cannot predict their amount. Please contact your local customs office for further information before placing your order.
