Who we are

As makers ourselves we were frustrated with the mounting costs of selling our products through the larger commercial selling websites. We thought it was time for someone to bring brilliant products to the market in a friendlier, more supportive way. We are creative designers who value brilliant ideas and want to share them with as many people as possible. Our fees are transparent and consistent so you know what the cost of selling will be upfront regardless of how many items you sell.

What we do

We work with artists, inventors, designers and makers to bring their brilliant products to market.

As a seller with a subscription you will be able to upload your photos, write the descriptions and set your own prices. You will communicate directly with your buyer and receive all the money paid (no additional fees taken by us, only transaction fees are debited from your total by the card payment gateway STRIPE). If your customer has a query or complaint you will deal with them directly. It is only if the buyer escalates the complaint, we at BIMTO will get involved. The seller is liable for ensuring their items are covered by insurance in the case of breakages or non-delivery. See full terms and conditions.

We have made it our mission to share the work of entrepreneurial, creative makers and artists with those who love things that are unique, functional and inspiring.

So often when trying to buy something unique, the market is flooded with things which look the same. This is our opportunity to give you gifts, solutions and ideas you hadn't even thought of.

Enjoy the experience of shopping in a different way and buying something unique and personal, a brilliant idea made to order.
